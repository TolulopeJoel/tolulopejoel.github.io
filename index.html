1. woohumore

WooHumore focuses on combating negativity bias in the news by creating a unique video-based news aggregator. This aggregator scours the web for both positive and negative news, presenting the positive impact or positive side of negative stories. This balanced approach aims to promote a more optimistic and informed news experience for people.

2. chunkit

Chunkit offers a secure and efficient solution by splitting files into smaller, manageable chunks. This tool tackles challenges like slow transfers, limited storage, and unreliable connections. Chunkit intelligently splits files, optimizes transfers, and can seamlessly merge splitted files back.
